Get in touch

Mentorship

As a mentor for kids in Instepanavan's running class, you'll have the opportunity to support the growth and development of young people in your community. This can be a rewarding and fulfilling experience that allows you to make a positive impact on the lives of young people and contribute to the success of Stepanavan. By joining Instepanavan's mentorship program, you'll be part of a community dedicated to supporting the growth and development of young people in Stepanavan.

Donate a computer

Donating a phone or PC to Instepanavan can make a positive impact on the community in Stepanavan, Armenia. Your donation can support educational initiatives, promote digital literacy, and reduce waste. Help make a difference in the lives of young people and the future of Stepanavan by donating a phone or PC.

Workshop

By doing a workshop with Instepanavan, you can share your knowledge and skills, support the growth and development of people in Stepanavan, connect with others, and learn and grow yourself. Participating in a workshop with Instepanavan can be a rewarding and fulfilling experience that allows you to make a positive impact on the community in Stepanavan, Armenia.
